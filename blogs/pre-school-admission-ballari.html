5 Things the Best Pre-Schools Should Offer

  • Play-based learning: Children learn through play — not rote memorisation.
  • Trained early childhood educators: Specialised training matters more than just experience.
  • Safe environment: CCTV, childproof classrooms, secure campus.
  • Low teacher ratio: Ideally 1 teacher for 10–12 toddlers.
  • Parent communication: Regular updates — not just report cards.
