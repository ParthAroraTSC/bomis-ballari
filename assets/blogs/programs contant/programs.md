# Our Educational Programmes - Birla Open Minds International School

## 1. Pre-School Programme
Birla Open Minds International School Preschool Curriculum celebrates the importance of these formative years by providing a strong and sure start.

### Key Features:
- **GEE TEE (Mascot):** Our mascot is a charismatic and clever bird standing tall and proud, ready to spread its wings and plunge into the high flight of creativity, collaboration, and innovation.
- **Early Years (Pre-Nursery to KG 2):** Our curriculum draws on neuroscientific research and is developmentally appropriate, integrated, and creative. It aims to instill care for self, for others, and for the environment.
- **Developmental Approach:** Our approach honors the development of the child as a whole: intellectually, physically, emotionally, and socially across ages.

### Learning Environment:
- **Creative Spaces:** Children are exposed to dynamic learning environments, challenges, and choices in activities.
- **Integrated Learning:** Theme-based curriculum that investigates ideas from multiple perspectives.
- **Learning Centres:** Resource-equipped centres where children engage in activities of their choice.
- **The Outdoors:** An extension of the classroom resplendent with vegetable patches, gardens, and mysteries of nature.

---

## 2. Primary School (Classes 1–5)
The Primary Years curriculum is carefully designed to nurture the child in all dimensions of his/her life to bring out the essence of a holistic being.

### Curriculum Highlights:
- **Holistic Development:** Fosters key skills in communication, problem-solving, citizenship, critical thinking, inquiry, and collaboration.
- **Individual Focus:** Takes into account individual uniqueness, personality types, and varied learning styles.
- **Thematic Approach:** Uses a thematic and integrated approach to value individuality.
- **Scholastic Subjects:** English Language Arts, Hindi, Third Language, Environmental Studies, Mathematics, and ICT.
- **Co-Scholastic Subjects:** Performing Arts, Visual Arts, Physical Education, Yoga, and Soul Science.

---

## 3. Middle School (Classes 6–8)
The Middle School curriculum amalgamates the curriculum of all major boards seamlessly, making it unique and rigorous.

### Hallmarks of the Program:
- **Student-Centric:** The onus of learning shifts from the teacher to the student, using active learning and inquiry-based methods.
- **Beyond Textbooks:** Focus on establishing a real-life connection with information rather than rote learning.
- **Subject Offerings:** English Language Arts, Hindi, Third Language, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and ICT.
- **SPEED Programme:** A specialized sports programme to develop skills and health awareness.
- **Soul Science:** Helps children deal with emotions, challenges, stress, and social insecurity.

### Assessment System:
- **Ongoing Assessments:** Regularly occurring throughout the instructional process using rubrics, checklists, and projects.
- **Term End Assessments:** Part of paper-pencil tests that require comprehension, analysis, and critical thinking.
